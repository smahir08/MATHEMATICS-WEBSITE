About

About the University

Overview

Established by the Government of Jammu & Kashmir through J&K Legislature Assembly Act No.V of 2002, Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University is mandated to impart quality education in basic and job oriented applied courses. The University, located at the base of Pir Panjal Mountain range, offers ideal ambience for teaching and learning.

The unique geographical location in rural setting offers state-of-the-art facilities even at the altitude of 1776 meters above sea level. The nearest airport(Jammu) and railway station(BGBS University, Baglihar) are 150km from Jammu and 20km from the University respectively.

About the Department

The Department of Mathematical Sciences is proud to be one of the oldest departments established at the University in 2005, playing a key role in the academic development.

The Department of Mathematics is a vibrant academic community of scholars dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and service. The mission is to provide students with a comprehensive education in mathematics, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, logical reasoning.

Department offers specialized programs in mathematics. Our curriculum is designed to cater to the diverse needs of students, from pure mathematics to applied mathematics in fields such as Functional Analysis, Operator Theory, Variational Analysis, Fixed Point Theory, Wavelet Analysis, Diff. Equations, Image Processing, Mathematical & Theoretical Biology.

Purpose

Objectives of the Conference

The main objective of this conference is to bring together senior and young researchers working in the areas of applicable analysis and its applications at the BGSB University which is located in the Pir panjal region of the Jammu and Kashmir and provide a platform to the local researchers to interact with researchers of international repute.

The topics include but are not limited to:

  • Fixed Point Theory
  • Variational Analysis
  • Fixed Point Theory
  • Numerical Analysis
  • Wavelet Analysis
  • Approximation Theory
  • Differential Equations
  • Complex Analysis
  • Graph Theory
  • Fuzzy Theory
  • Optimization
  • Financila Matheamtics
  • Data Science & Analysis
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Bio Mathematics
